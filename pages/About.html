Arpis Pharmaceutical Pvt. Ltd., founded in 2024 in Mumbai, is dedicated to leveraging science to enhance lives with vibrancy. With cutting-edge manufacturing facilities in Baddi, Himachal Pradesh, we have established a robust foothold in Bihar, Jharkhand, U.P., Maharashtra, and New Delhi through our own offices and distribution channels. Our workforce is sizable and committed.

Our growth strategy revolves around cultivating a diverse portfolio of high-quality products and broadening our geographical footprint. Arpis is passionately committed to pioneering improved and cost-effective treatment options, poised to positively impact millions of lives.
